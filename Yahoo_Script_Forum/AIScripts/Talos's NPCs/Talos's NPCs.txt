Talos's NPCs  ver 2. 12/21/00

I added another NPC merchant for your enjoyment. A Taxidermist that sells stuffed animal heads. 

Package includes:

Florest:	Sells flowers
Taxidermist:	Sells animal trophy.

_____________
To install:

copy itemdesc.cfg contents to your Pol\config\itemdesc.cfg file
copy mrcspawn.cfg contents to your Pol\config\mrcspawn.cfg file
copy npcdesc.cfg contents to your Pol\config\npcdesc.cfg file
